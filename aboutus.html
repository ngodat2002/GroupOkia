Our Environment

We are barely perfect when it comes to our environment, but we will prioritize providing better information and taking the necessary steps to reduce Marudryfruits.com's impact on the environment. also recycle all of our cardboard boxes. As for lighting, we have replaced HID (metal halide) lamp with energy saving fluorescent lamp. These new lamps consume nearly 60% less energy. We also mount motion sensors so that when we're not working in the warehouse area, the lights go out and save even more. It was a beginning. But we have a long way to go.

Our Mission Statement

Marudryfruits.com established as the leading supplier of the best dried fruits and nuts in the world while maintaining our principles of zero tolerance as we evolved. The following six guiding principles will help us measure the appropriateness of our decisions: -Provide a great working environment and treat each other with respect and dignity. -Take diversity as an essential ingredient in the way we do business. -Apply the highest standards of excellence to the purchasing, roasting and distribution of our fresh nuts. -Enthusiastic development to satisfy customers at all times. -Make a positive contribution to our communities and our environment. -Realizing that profitability is essential to our future success.
